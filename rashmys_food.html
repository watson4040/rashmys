About Rashmy's Food

Rashmy's Food started as a small family kitchen in Lusaka, serving authentic homemade meals. Today, we are a beloved local spot known for our warm hospitality, fresh ingredients, and fusion of Zambian and international flavors.

We offer dine-in, takeaway, and free delivery within Lusaka for orders above K150. Our mission is to make every meal feel like home.

Location: 14 Great East Road, Lusaka (near Arcades)

Hours: Mon-Sun 11am – 10pm
